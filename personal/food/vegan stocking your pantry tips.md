## Common Ingredients
- Tahini (paste made from sesame seeds)
	- mild in flavor
	- slightly nutty
	- should be pourable
	- used in humus
	- top sweet or savory dishes with it
	- Hulled (soaked before ground, lighter in color) and unhulled (thicker ground whole) tahini
